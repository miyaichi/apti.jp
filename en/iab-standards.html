IAB Tech Lab Standards - Japanese Translations

To promote understanding of ads.txt, sellers.json, and SupplyChain Object specifications, APTI provides Japanese translations of official IAB Tech Lab documents. These standards are essential technical specifications for building a transparent and healthy advertising supply chain.

※ These translations are provided for understanding purposes. Please always refer to the official IAB Tech Lab documents for authoritative specifications.

IAB Tech Lab Standards - Japanese Translation List

1. ads.txt Version 1.1

A text file specification for declaring authorized sellers. Publishers can specify their authorized sellers to prevent ad fraud and improve transparency in the advertising supply chain.

Original PDF Japanese Translation PDF

2. Authorized Sellers for Apps (app-ads.txt)

The ads.txt specification for mobile applications. It clarifies authorized seller relationships in in-app advertising and ensures transparency in app advertising.

Original PDF Japanese Translation PDF

3. ads.txt Implementation Guide

A practical guide for implementing ads.txt. It comprehensively covers technical implementation methods and operational best practices.

Original PDF Japanese Translation PDF

4. Sellers.json

A JSON specification for ad tech providers to publish seller information. It works in conjunction with ads.txt to achieve transparency across the entire supply chain.

Original PDF Japanese Translation PDF

5. FAQ for sellers.json and SupplyChain Object

A collection of frequently asked questions about sellers.json and SupplyChain Object. Helpful for resolving implementation questions and promoting understanding.

Original PDF Japanese Translation PDF

Contributing to Translations

These translations are collaborative efforts by APTI participating members. If you have suggestions for translation improvements or wish to request translations of new documents, please join us and participate in our community.

Translation Principles

  • Prioritize technical accuracy while ensuring practical usability
  • Maintain the structure and intent of the original while making it naturally readable in Japanese
  • Consistently translate technical terms, providing English terms when necessary
  • Add supplementary explanations as needed to enhance implementer understanding

Sharing These Resources

We encourage sharing these Japanese translations with Japanese-speaking colleagues, partners, and stakeholders in the advertising industry. APTI's mission is to promote transparency and best practices across the entire ecosystem, and these translations serve as valuable resources for the Japanese advertising technology community.

These resources demonstrate APTI's commitment to fostering international collaboration and knowledge sharing in building transparent advertising supply chains.
